Phishing, Vishing, and Smishing Module

Step 5 of 5

Conclusion

Phishing, vishing, and smishing are all dangerous types of social engineering scams that can result in identity theft, financial loss, and other security risks. Understanding these threats and practicing caution when dealing with unsolicited communications can help you avoid becoming a victim. By staying alert, verifying information, and reporting scams, you can protect yourself and others from these online threats.
