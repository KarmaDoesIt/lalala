What is Phishing, Vishing, and Smishing?

Phishing, vishing, and smishing are types of online scams that trick people into giving away their personal information. These attacks are designed to appear legitimate, often coming from trusted sources, to make victims believe they’re taking necessary actions when, in fact, they’re being deceived. These scams can lead to identity theft, financial loss, or damage to one’s personal security.
