How to Handle Smishing, Vishing, and Phising

  1. Don’t Share Personal Information: – Never give out your personal information over the phone, email, or text unless you’re sure it’s a legitimate request. Real companies will never ask for passwords, social security numbers, or bank details this way.
  2. Report the Scam: – If you’ve received a suspicious email, phone call, or text, report it to the company or organization being impersonated. Many companies have hotlines or email addresses for reporting scams. You can also report phishing attempts to your local authorities or use websites like ReportPhishing@apwg.org.
  3. Use Antivirus Software: – Make sure you have updated antivirus software running on your devices. This software can help identify malicious emails or links before you interact with them.
