What Does a Computer Virus Do?

Computer viruses come in many forms and serve different malicious purposes. Some of the common effects of a virus include:

  • Data Corruption - Viruses may delete or alter files, making them unusable.
  • System Slowdown - Many viruses consume system resources, causing computers to lag.
  • Unauthorized Access - Some viruses allow hackers to gain control of a system.
  • Spreading to Other Devices - Certain viruses replicate through USB drives, networks, or email attachments.
  • Ransomware Attacks - Some modern viruses, like ransomware, lock users out of their files until a ransom is paid (Europol, 2021).
