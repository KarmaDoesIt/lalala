How to Prevent Mobile Malware

Prevention is Key!

  • Download Apps from Trusted Sources: Only download apps from official app stores, like Google Play Store or Apple’s App Store. Avoid third-party sites, as they may offer apps with hidden malware.
  • Read App Permissions: Always check the permissions an app requests before downloading. If an app asks for more permissions than it needs, like access to your contacts, camera, or messages, it could be suspicious.
  • Keep Your Device Updated: Regular updates to your phone’s operating system and apps are important because they include security patches that fix vulnerabilities malware could exploit.
  • Install Antivirus or Anti-Malware Apps: Use trusted antivirus software designed for mobile devices to detect and remove malicious apps or files. These apps can scan your phone for potential threats.
