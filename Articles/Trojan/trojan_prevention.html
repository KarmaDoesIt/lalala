How to Combat and Remove Trojans

Preventing Trojan Infections

  • Download Software from Official Sources – Avoid third-party websites or torrents that may distribute infected files.
  • Use Strong Security Software – Antivirus programs like Norton, Malwarebytes, and Windows Defender can detect and block Trojans.
  • Be Wary of Email Attachments & Links – Phishing emails often contain Trojans disguised as invoices, receipts, or job offers.
  • Keep Your Software Updated – Security patches help fix vulnerabilities that Trojans exploit.
