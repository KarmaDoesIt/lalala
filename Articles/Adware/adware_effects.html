What Does Adware do to a Computer?

Once installed, adware can cause several issues, including:

  1. Intrusive Pop-Up Ads - Unwanted ads appear frequently, disrupting browsing and reducing productivity.
  2. Browswer Hijacking - Some adware changes browser settings, redirecting users to shady websites.
  3. System Slowdowns - Excessive ads and background processes consume memory and CPU power.
  4. Data Collection & Privacy Risks - Some adware tracks browsing habits and sells user data to advertisers or cybercriminals (McAfee, 2022).
  5. Increased Security Risks - Clicking on adware-generated pop-ups may lead to additional malware infections, such as spyware or ransomware.
